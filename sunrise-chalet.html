About This Space

Step Straight Onto the Slopes

NEW: Private hot tub on the deck! Located in the newly developed resort area on Hudson Bay Mountain, this stunning ski-in/ski-out cabin lets you step straight onto the slopes from your front door and ski directly to the chairlift.

The open-concept layout features a spacious, fully equipped kitchen that flows into a cozy living area with glass doors leading out to a large deck showcasing breathtaking valley views.

With four bedrooms and two bathrooms spread across two levels, there's plenty of space for 2–3 families to enjoy a comfortable mountain getaway together.

The Space. Thoughtfully set up for your stay with fresh sheets, pillows, towels, and all the kitchen essentials.

Guest Access. Enjoy true ski-in/ski-out convenience from the doorstep. The cabin is located in the new Hudson Bay Mountain resort development. Check the Hudson Bay Mountain website for current conditions and lift hours. You'll receive your personalized door code prior to arrival.
