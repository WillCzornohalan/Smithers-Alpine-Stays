About Smithers Alpine Stays

Mountain Cabin Rentals in Beautiful British Columbia

Self-contained ski-in/ski-out cabins nestled on Hudson Bay Mountain and tucked into peaceful corners of the surrounding forest — offering year-round access to nature's best in Smithers, BC.

Your ideal base for adventure in every season: carve down snowy slopes in winter, explore scenic hiking and mountain biking trails in summer, unwind in your private hot tub, spot wildlife, paddle across glacial lakes, gather around a crackling wood stove, or simply soak in the breathtaking mountain views.

Whatever your pace, the mountain always calls you back.
