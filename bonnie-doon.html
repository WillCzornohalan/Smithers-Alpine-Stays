About This Space

Escape to Nature on Hudson Bay Mountain

Tucked among the trees on Hudson Bay Mountain, this cozy cabin offers a peaceful escape with ski-in, ski-out access and all the comforts of home. Just a 20-minute drive from the town of Smithers, it's the perfect retreat for nature lovers, families, and mountain adventurers alike.

The spacious home includes 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, a large open kitchen, and a comfortable lounge with a wood-burning fireplace — ideal for accommodating up to three families. The basement features a second lounge and kitchenette, giving everyone room to spread out.

Enjoy the fully equipped kitchen (including a dishwasher, Tassimo coffee machine, and basic pantry items), or fire up the gas BBQ on the front deck. The back deck features a fire pit and seating, great for evening gatherings.

Location & Access

Ski directly to the chairlift

The cabin is part of a new development on Hudson Bay Mountain. You can ski directly from the property down to the chairlift. For current snow conditions and lift times, check the Hudson Bay Mountain website.
