About This Space

Scandinavian-Inspired Luxury with Panoramic Views

Kick back and unwind in this calm, stylish mountain retreat. Located slope-side with true ski-in, ski-out access, this Scandinavian-inspired home blends modern design with cozy alpine charm — perfect for couples or a group of friends seeking comfort, style, and space.

After a day on the slopes, soak in the private hot tub overlooking breathtaking valley views. The open-concept living room features a wood-burning stove, plush furnishings, and floor-to-ceiling windows that bring the outdoors in. The fully equipped kitchen and elegant dining area make shared meals a delight.

Each of the three king-sized bedrooms includes a private ensuite, offering privacy and luxury for all guests. A large, welcoming boot room makes gearing up for ski days effortless, while the basement offers access to a laundry room and a ski tuning bench for added convenience.

Whether you're here for fresh powder or a peaceful mountain retreat, this home is your base for high-end relaxation on Hudson Bay Mountain.
