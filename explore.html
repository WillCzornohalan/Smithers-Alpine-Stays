Winter

Skiing & Snowboarding

Skiing and snowboarding on Hudson Bay Mountain offers an unforgettable alpine experience right from your doorstep. With over 300 acres of skiable terrain, the mountain features groomed runs, powder-filled glades, and steep chutes for every level. Ski-in/ski-out access from our cabins means you can hit the slopes in minutes and enjoy stunning views of the Bulkley Valley all day long. Consistent snowfall, uncrowded lift lines, and a laid-back mountain vibe make Hudson Bay a true hidden gem of northern BC.

Summer

Mountain Biking

Mountain biking in Smithers is a thrilling ride through the stunning landscapes of the Bulkley Valley. Trails range from smooth beginner routes to technical descents, with networks at Bluff, Piper, and Ptarmigan Recreation Sites catering to all skill levels. On and around Hudson Bay Mountain, riders enjoy flowy berms, wooden features, and breathtaking alpine views. The riding season runs May to November, with trails maintained by the Smithers Mountain Bike Association.

Year-round

Hiking

Hiking around Hudson Bay Mountain and the Smithers area offers some of the most scenic trails in northern BC. From alpine ridge walks with panoramic views to peaceful forest paths and waterfall hikes, there's something for every pace and ability. Trails like Crater Lake, Silver King Basin, and Twin Falls take you through breathtaking terrain filled with wildflowers, wildlife, and crisp mountain air. Whether you want a quick morning walk or a full-day adventure, the trails are endless and just minutes from your cabin door.

Local

Cafés, Restaurants & Bars

Smithers offers a delightful array of local cafés, restaurants, and bars. Start your day at Two Sisters Café — a cozy spot known for homemade pastries and aromatic coffee. For craft beer, Smithers Brewing Co. is a must-visit, with inventive beers like the Hudson Bay ISA and Twin Falls Pale Ale brewed on-site. The Roadhouse offers internationally inspired comfort food made from locally sourced ingredients, with a curated list of premium whiskies, rums, and BC-centric wines.
